Provides individual font faces for the `Adobe Clean` font family.

### Component usage

Font faces are loaded on demand. Each font class needs to be added somewhere
in the entry point in order to use a specific variant. Use the `Adobe Clean`
font family, or any of the exported class names to style text blocks

```jsx live
<div>
  <AdobeCleanBlack />
  <AdobeCleanBoldIt />
  <AdobeCleanExtraBold />
  <AdobeCleanExtraBoldIt />
  <AdobeCleanIt />
  <AdobeCleanLight />
  <AdobeCleanLightIt />
  <AdobeClean />
  <AdobeCleanMedium />
  <AdobeCleanMediumIt />
  <AdobeCleanSemiLight />
  <AdobeCleanSemiLightIt />
  <div style={{ fontFamily: 'Adobe Clean' }}>
    <p>
      <strong>abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz</strong>
      <br />
      <em>ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ</em>
      <br />
      <span style={{ fontWeight: 300 }}>
        0123456789.:,;()*!?'@#&lt;&gt;$%&amp;^+-=~
      </span>
    </p>
    <p className={light}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={lightIt}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={semiLight}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={semiLightIt}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={normal}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={medium}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={it}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={boldIt}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={extraBold}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={black}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p className={blackIt}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
  </div>
</div>
```
